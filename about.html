Who We Are

Crafted for the Connoisseur

Stogie Candles are a carefully composed collection of scents inspired by the rich, complex aromas of fine cigars aged tobacco, dark cedar, bourbon, sandalwood, and leather. Each candle is crafted to fill a room with the kind of atmosphere that commands attention.

Whether you're a cigar aficionado or simply drawn to deep, sophisticated scents, Stogie Candles belong in your space.

How It Started

Born in the Lounge

Our journey began in the heart of a traditional cigar lounge where the air was thick with aged tobacco, bourbon, leather, and oak. We wanted to capture that atmosphere and bring the weight of it into homes, offices, and every space worth owning.

Each candle is hand-poured with premium soy wax and infused with fragrance oils that evoke the timeless ritual of a fine stogie and a glass of something aged.

Our Craft

The Art of the Stogie

From the bold, commanding presence of The Chairman to the smooth sophistication of The Consigliere every candle tells a story. We've spent years perfecting our blends, working with master perfumers to capture not just tobacco, but the full sensory world of a luxury cigar lounge: bourbon, cedar, dark leather, smoked oak, and amber.

100% natural soy wax. Lead-free cotton wicks. Phthalate-free fragrance oils. Each candle burns 50–60 hours. No shortcuts. No mass production. Just craft.
